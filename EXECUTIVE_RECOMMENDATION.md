### PRIORITY 2: OPTIMIZE OR EXIT LOW RISK SEGMENT

**Action:** Reduce CAC for Low Risk from $92 to <$50, or reallocate budget

**Rationale:**
- 2.47x LTV/CAC is below 3x minimum threshold
- Only 3.7% of revenue despite 10.3% of loans
- Poor contribution margin at 38.5%
- Inefficient use of acquisition capital

**Options:**

**Option A - Optimize CAC:**
- Target CAC reduction to $45 (from $92)
- Would improve LTV/CAC to 5.0x (profitable)
- Focus on organic/referral channels
- Test lower-cost acquisition methods

**Option B - Reallocate Budget:**
- Redirect $26k CAC investment to High Risk
- Expected additional return: $270k revenue
- Net improvement: +$244k vs current Low Risk revenue

**Recommendation:** Attempt Option A for Q2, execute Option B if CAC cannot be reduced by 50%.

---

### PRIORITY 3: MAXIMIZE CUSTOMER RETENTION

**Action:** Launch repeat borrower incentive program

**Rationale:**
- 45.7% current retention rate
- Repeat customers: -43% delinquency vs first-time
- Zero marginal CAC on repeat loans = infinite ROI
- Average 4.7 loans per retained customer

**Program Design:**

1. **Loyalty Tiers:**
   - Loan 2-3: -0.5% APR discount
   - Loan 4-5: -1.0% APR discount
   - Loan 6+: -1.5% APR discount + priority approval

2. **Reactivation Campaign:**
   - Target customers 90+ days since last loan
   - Email/SMS with pre-approved offers
   - Simplified application process

3. **Referral Incentives:**
   - $10 credit for referrer and referee
   - Stackable up to 5 referrals

**Expected Impact:**
- Increase retention from 45.7% to 60% (+14.3pp)
- Generate +643 additional loans in Q2
- Add +$110k revenue with zero CAC

**Budget:** $15k (0.7% of Q1 revenue) for incentive program

---

### PRIORITY 4: IMPROVE MEDIUM RISK ECONOMICS

**Action:** Reduce CAC for Medium Risk from $71 to $50

**Rationale:**
- Current 5.85x LTV/CAC is acceptable but improvable
- Represents 29.7% of revenue (significant volume)
- CAC reduction of 30% would improve LTV/CAC to 8.3x

**Tactics:**
- Shift from paid advertising to content marketing
- Implement referral program (see Priority 3)
- Optimize conversion funnel (reduce drop-off)
- Test partnerships with employers/associations

**Target:** $50 CAC by end of Q2 2025

**Expected Impact:**
- Improve LTV/CAC from 5.85x to 8.3x
- Save $28k in Q2 acquisition costs
- Maintain or grow Medium Risk volume
